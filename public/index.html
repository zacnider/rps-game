Rock Paper Scissors is a simple game played between two players. Each player chooses one of three options: Rock, Paper, or Scissors. The outcomes are:

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Paper beats Rock
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • If both players choose the same option, it's a draw

Each win gives you 5 points, each loss deducts 5 points, and a draw gives 0 points. Have fun!
